How the projection is built
E[HRR] = expected_PA × (blended per-PA H/R/RBI rates) × Quality × Hand × Team × Park × Weather × Bullpen × BvP. P(O1.5) = P(HRR ≥ 2) and P(O2.5) = P(HRR ≥ 3) via Poisson with that expected value. Edge = best P(over) − 52.4% (break-even at −110 juice). Per-PA rates are blended from full-season and last-15-day rates using shrinkage toward season (weight = recent_PA / (recent_PA + 100)).
